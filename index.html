BHS Calculator

Software Tool | 2023

BHS Calculator is a specialized tool developed to calculate and verify compliance with building standards according to BHS (Building Health Standards) regulations.

Main Features

  • Automatic calculation of ventilation and natural lighting ratios
  • Verification of compliance with local and international regulations
  • Generation of detailed reports in PDF format
  • Intuitive interface with 3D visualization of results

Platform
Windows Desktop Application
Technologies

Integrations
Revit, AutoCAD, Excel

Dummy Flow

Simulation System | 2022

Dummy Flow is a pedestrian flow simulation system designed to optimize circulation in complex architectural spaces.

Applications

  • Circulation analysis in transportation stations
  • Layout optimization in shopping centers
  • Emergency evacuation planning
  • Design of safe and efficient public spaces
Multi-agent Simulation

Modeling of individual and collective behaviors

Data Analysis

Density, speed and travel time metrics

3D Visualization

Immersive representation of pedestrian flows

UCL Bartlett Research Project

Academic Research | 2021-2022

Research project developed during my stay at the prestigious University College London (UCL), Bartlett School of Architecture, focused on the application of artificial intelligence for the generation of optimized architectural forms.

Research Objectives

  • Develop generative algorithms for architectural design
  • Optimize structural forms through machine learning
  • Reduce material consumption while maintaining structural integrity
  • Create accessible tools for architects and designers

Derived Publications

Luce, E. (2022). "Generative Design for Structural Optimization: A Machine Learning Approach". Journal of Architectural Research, 15(2), 45-62.
